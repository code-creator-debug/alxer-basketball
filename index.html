Alxer

About Us

Alxer is more than just a club—it's a movement. We believe every child, no matter where, deserves safe and inspiring basketball spaces. We are a semi-nonprofit initiative that combines sport, hope, and community impact.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: Build basketball courts for schools in Africa that currently have none. Through donations, multilingual outreach, and a global network of supporters, Alxer empowers youth, one hoop at a time.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate players, coaches, and volunteers worldwide who share a common dream—to bring hope through basketball. We work together to build courts that become homes for talent and growth.

Languages

Alxer supports multiple languages to connect with supporters globally. Stay tuned for updates on available translations.
